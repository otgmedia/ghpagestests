We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when using our Games, which may include:

Contact Information: If you contact us through the Game, you may provide your email address or other contact information.

When you use our Games, certain information is automatically collected, including:

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Communication: We may send you important notices, updates, and promotional materials (if you have opted in). Some of these promotional materials may be provided through ad networks like AdMob and AppLovin Max.
